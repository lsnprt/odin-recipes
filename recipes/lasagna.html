Lasagna

Description

While it takes some prep time, this vegetarian lasagna will win over even the most die-hard meat fans!

Ingredients

  • 500 grams vegetarian Bolognese sauce (recipe coming soon!)
  • 1 litre bechamel sauce
  • 120 grams grated parmesan cheese
  • 500 grams fresh lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 225#8451.
  2. On a lightly greased tray, lay alternating layers of lasagna sheets, bechamel, Bolognese sauce, and parmesan
  3. Repeat the previous step 4-5 times or until you run out of ingredients (Lasagna is not an exact science)
  4. Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes. Once ready take it out of the oven and let it cool for at least 10 minutes.
  5. Buon Appetito!
