Pizza

Description

Here's my favourite recipe for a flavourful and crispy pizza that doesn't need to rise very long and always delivers. The recipe is for two medium sized pizzas

Ingredients

  • 350 grams wheat flour (Tipo 00 works best)
  • 7 grams dried yeast
  • 230 milliliters lager beer (Alcohol-free works perfect, it's about the flavour!)
  • 5 grams of salt
  • 40 milliliters extra virgin olive oil
  • 200 milliliters good quality tomato sauce
  • 80 grams mozzarella, diced into small cubes
  • Optional: there's no rules in pizza, go nuts on your favourite toppings

Steps

  1. In a bowl, blend flour, yeast, and salt. Stir well.
  2. Add the beer and olive oil. Mix well, first with a spoon, then with your hands, until you have a malleable dough.
  3. Knead the dough for approximately 15 minutes.
  4. Shape the dough into two buns, cover them with a wet cloth, then set aside to raise for at least one and a half hour.
  5. Preheat the oven at 250℃. In the meantime, shape the two buns into pizzas onto some parchment paper.
  6. Add the tomato sauce, then the mozzarella and other topping of your choice.
  7. Bake the pizzas in the oven for approximately 10 minutes.
  8. Buon appetito!
