Peanut Soup

Description

This African peanut soup is made with a wonderful combination of ingredients for a surprisingly different and delicious soup that everyone will enjoy! Serve with hot sauce and sweet potatoes or rice on the side

Ingredients

  • 400 grams kale, chopped
  • One large red onion, chopped
  • 1,5 litres of vegetable stock
  • Two garlic cloves, minced
  • 10 grams of ginger, grated
  • 80 grams tomato paste
  • 200 grams chunky peanut butter
  • Salt, pepper, and any hot sauce to taste

Steps

  1. Bring the vegetable stock to a boil, add the onion, garlic, and ginger, then lower the temperature and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, combine 0,5 of the hot stock with the peanut butter and tomato paste until smooth. Add the mixture back into the simmering stock.
  3. Add the kale, then let the soup simmer for another 15 minutes.
  4. Season, with salt, pepper, and hot sauce (In my case I used Sriracha and that worked amazingly)
  5. Buon Appetito!
